IMPORTANT REMINDER

Reduce Your Policy Deductible Before Policy Start Date

Dear Client,

I hope this email finds you well. As your trusted insurance advisor, I wanted to send you a timely reminder regarding your policy deductible.

**Before your policy officially starts**, it's crucial to ask our office staff to **reduce your policy deductible** if you wish to do so. Once the policy is in effect, the deductible amount cannot be altered. This means that if you require medical attention, you would be responsible for covering costs up to the amount of your policy deductible for each instance.

To put it simply, if you have a higher deductible and seek medical care, you might need to pay a significant amount out-of-pocket before your insurance coverage kicks in.

If you decide to reduce your deductible, please note that you will be required to **pay the difference in the new policy amount**. This adjustment ensures that your coverage reflects the lower deductible.

To avoid any potential financial strain, please take a moment to review your policy details and decide if you'd like to reduce your deductible. If you choose to make this change, please reach out to our office staff at your earliest convenience and **before the policy start date**.

Thank you for your attention to this matter. We're here to ensure you have the best coverage that suits your needs. If you have any questions or require further clarification, please don't hesitate to contact us.
