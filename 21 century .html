Are you planning your next trip? Whether it’s a quick visit or an extended stay, 21st Century Insurance offers the travel medical coverage you need to stay protected.

Here’s What You Need to Know:
Easy Eligibility:

  • Available for travelers under 86 years old
  • No medical declaration needed if you're under 55
  • Standard and Enhanced Plans for those 55–85 based on health answers


Reliable Coverage:

  • Emergency medical benefits tailored to your age and health status
  • Fast claims support when you need it most


What’s Not Covered:

  • Pre-existing conditions (varies by plan)
  • High-risk activities like rock climbing, parachuting, or pro sports
  • Pregnancy-related care or complications close to your due date
  • Treatments not related to emergencies
