Universal Life Insurance

This life insurance offers flexibility to the policyholder. Therefore, it is also known as Adjustable life Insurance.

  

The beauty of this policy lies in the fact that the following premium rates may be different from the first, that is, the rates can be reduced as per the requirements. This is a great coverage insurance that provides protection to your family after your death.

  

You have the liberty to pay the amount either on a monthly basis or as a lump sum amount before the stipulated time. Also, in case of a financial crisis, the cash value system of this policy comes to play, wherein you use the face value of the policy to pay your other premiums.

  

The coverage plan also comes with a flexible maturity date which around the time you turn 85 years and can be extended upto 120 years. Our experts will be more than happy to lay the perfect policy for you once you come to us for guidance.
