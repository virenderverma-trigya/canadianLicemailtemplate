Money Back Life Insurance

In case of a Money Back Life Insurance Plan, the policy holder keeps getting a certain percentage of the assured amount at regular intervals.

 

In case you want to save by having an insurance plan and enough money in hand, i.e. maintain liquidity throughout, this coverage is the best for you. You get to  set the term period for this policy that can be 10, 20 or 30 years. In case you live more than the years set as the policy term, you will have to apply for a new coverage plan.

  

Our team of expert advisors will guide you through the policy plans that would best suit you. You will only have to invest some money in this policy and this will be enough to give you a secured future. You should definitely go for this coverage if you are not good at saving money but still want to have a protected financial life. In case of death of the insured person, the nominee gets the entire sum assured and the survival benefits are not deducted.
