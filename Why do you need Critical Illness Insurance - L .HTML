Why do you need Critical Illness Insurance?

Critical illness does not end in hospital. Also, there are other health care expenses which are not covered by health cards.

  

Once Someone diagnosed with some kind of critical illness, their abilities to do work reduces but the payment of all the bills and other expenses does not stop.

  

If a person is diagnosed with any of the below mentioned 24 Critical Illnesses, he/she will not be able to go to work from 6 to 12 months. In that period of recovery from the illness, the Expenses like Hospital Room, Equipment cost like wheelchair, Ramp for  wheelchair, Stairlift, Daily medicines, External help to do daily tasks can be paid by the cash benefits you get from the Insurance.

  

We can help you to cover the other expenses with critical illness insurance. This insurance provides coverage if you experience one or more of the following medical emergencies:

  • Heart attack
  • Stroke
  • Cancer
  • Coronary bypass
  • Kidney Failure
  • Paralysis
  • Blindness
  • Alzheimer’s Disease
  • Parkinson’s Disease
  • Motor Neuron/Lou Gehrig’s Disease
  • Major/Vital Organ Failure/Transplant
  • Multiple Sclerosis
  • Loss of Speech
  • Severe Burns
  • Severe Burns
  • Coma
  • Loss Of Limbs
  • Occupational HIV
  • Benign Brain Tumour
  • Loss of Independence
  • Aortic Surgery
  • Heart Valve Replacement
  • Aplastic Anaemia
  • Bacterial Meningitis.
