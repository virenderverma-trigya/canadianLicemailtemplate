In Supervisa/Visitor/Travel insurance premiums vary based upon the traveler's age. Typically, insurance companies adjust premiums based on age brackets because different age groups can have different levels of risk.


As per most of the companies, policy premium is based upon age of the person as per the start date.


If the traveler's age changes before they travel, the insurance provider may recalculate the premium based on the new age bracket, which could either increase or decrease the cost.


Once the policy is active, the premium will remain the same for the full period.


BUT if the policy isn’t activated yet and waiting for future activation, then please be prepared that whenever we are changing the start date, the premium may increase if their age is increased.


For example, at the time of application insured age is 60, but as per policy start date, insured’s age is 61, then premium will change and difference needs to be paid.
