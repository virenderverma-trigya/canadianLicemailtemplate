TFSA

A Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) is an account with tax benefits. In short, there are no taxes applied on any contributions, interest earned, capital gains or dividends. All your investments become tax-free and the amount withdrawn is tax-free as well. You even have the option of depositing the money back into the TFSA, preferably the following year.

Individuals aged 18 years and above in Canada are eligible for this particular savings account. In some provinces, individuals need to be at least 19 years old to be able to open an account.
